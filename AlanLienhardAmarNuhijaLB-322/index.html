Our Journey

Welcome to Amar & Alan's Coffee Shop, where every cup tells a story. Nestled in the heart of Japan, Kame House, we are more than just a coffee shop – we are a cozy corner for friends, a quiet space for thinkers, and a lively meeting place for the community.

Our journey began with a simple love for coffee and a passion for bringing people together. At Amar & Alan's Coffee Shop, we believe in the magic of the perfect brew. That’s why we carefully source our beans from the finest farms around the world, roast them locally to perfection, and craft each cup with care. Whether you’re here for a morning pick-me-up, an afternoon escape, or an evening wind-down, we aim to serve coffee that inspires.

But it’s not just about the coffee. Our space is designed to feel like your second home – warm, inviting, and filled with good vibes. Enjoy a cozy nook with a book, savor a fresh pastry from our local bakery partners, or simply take a moment to soak in the atmosphere. Come in, stay awhile, and be part of our story. Because at Amar & Alan's Coffee Shop, it’s always about good coffee, great people, and creating moments that matter. Welcome to your new favorite spot. We can’t wait to brew something special for you.
